The Problem

Trillions frozen in fields, not banks

570M
Smallholder farmers globally locked out of credit
$1.5T
Annual agricultural receivables sitting unpaid
60%
Of farmer income delayed 90–180 days post-harvest
No credit history. No collateral. No underwriter. Liquidity dies in the field.
The Solution

A machine-readable credit market for agriculture

AgriTrust registers crop invoices as on-chain assets, grades them with a deterministic AI underwriter, and lets liquidity providers fund them instantly — settled on Casper.
📋

Register

Farmer tokenizes a receivable (commodity, region, amount, maturity) as an on-chain invoice on Casper.

🤖

Evaluate

AI agent scores risk deterministically from region × commodity × amount, buying verification data via x402.

💰

Fund & Settle

Liquidity providers fund graded deals at a discount. On maturity, settlement executes on-chain.
