The Problem

570 million farmers locked out of credit — because their data is off-chain.

Most of the world's smallholder farmers have no access to affordable working capital. They sell standing crops for a fraction of their worth just to stay solvent.

The farmers are creditworthy — predictable yields, growing demand, crop insurance — but that data lives scattered across weather APIs, market-price feeds, and co-op ledgers. Traditional finance can't stitch it together cheaply enough for a $400 invoice.

AgriTrust closes that gap with an autonomous agent that buys data, underwrites risk, and deploys capital — all on-chain.

Built for the x402 Economy

Three technologies, one autonomous credit pipeline.

🤖

AI Underwriting Agent

An autonomous agent queries weather APIs, satellite NDVI, market-price feeds, and farmer KYC data — synthesizing them into a deterministic on-chain risk verdict (score, band, max advance, discount rate) in seconds.

Agentic × On-Chain
⚡

x402 Micropayments

Every data check is paid per-request via HTTP 402 + secp256k1 signatures. No subscriptions, no API keys — the agent drops a fraction of a cent and gets the data. Costs tracked on-chain as x402_spent.

Pay-per-byte
📄

RWA Invoice Tokenization

A farmer's harvest becomes an on-chain invoice with commodity, region, face value, and maturity. The contract tracks its full lifecycle: Registered → Evaluated → Funded → Settled / Defaulted.
